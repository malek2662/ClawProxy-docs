## Reporting an Issue

When reporting an issue, please include:

1. **ClawProxy version** (`clawproxy --version`)
2. **Operating system** (Linux, macOS, or Windows)
3. **The exact error message or HTTP status code**
4. **Steps to reproduce the issue**
5. **Relevant log entries** (from the Logs page or `clawproxy logs`)

This information helps diagnose and resolve your issue as quickly as possible.

---

## Activation Support

If you need help with activation:
1. Copy your **Installation ID** from the Awaiting Activation screen.
2. Send it to the developer via email or Reddit.
3. Your installation will be activated promptly.

> The Installation ID is machine-specific. If you move to a new machine, you will need a new activation.
